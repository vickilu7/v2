Lose the Snooze

Role: Programming and UI/UX Design
Collaborators: Faraz Yashar & Angela Moon

Many alarms allow us to hit snooze as many times as we wish. We've created a simple alarm clock app that will actually wake you up. This app was created as part of the New Member Education process for Catalyst (Duke social and pre-professional tech organization) and was coded on XCode using JavaScript.
