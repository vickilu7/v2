Product Design Internship
Time: 12 weeks

During Summer 2019 I had the opportunity to intern with the GlaxoSmithKline Platforms team. I can’t show work from my projects or give any details due to NDA, but here’s some of what I worked on throughout the internship:

• A skills progression tool for professional development within teams
• A design system that streamlines the product development process

Process

Skills Progression Tool

For my intern project, I started with conceptualizing and exploring different directions. Through user research practices and design reviews, I iterated and was able to bring a proof-of-concept to life with wireframe flows, detailed hi-fidelity mocks, and clickthrough prototypes.

Design System

This team project involved working closely with our Brand team and I'm glad I had the opportunity to be a part of the design system's first phase. I helped out with user validation testing, conducted external audits, defined a grid system, and wrote documentation for the system file use.
